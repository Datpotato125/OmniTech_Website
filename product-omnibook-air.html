Key Features

Light. Fast. Silent.

Performance

OmniChip M1 — Silent Power

Completely fanless architecture means OmniBook Air operates in total silence. OmniChip M1's unified memory architecture delivers performance that rivals multi-fan designs at 45W TDP.

Battery

22-Hour Battery Life

The most battery life ever in an OmniTech laptop. Fly across the Atlantic and still have hours of charge left. OmniOS power management adjusts per-process energy draw in real time.

Portability

1.24 kg Unibody

Machined from a single recycled aluminum billet, OmniBook Air weighs less than a hardcover book. The 11.3 mm profile slides into any bag without a second thought.

Display

Liquid Retina IPS

P3 wide color and True Tone display adapts color temperature to ambient light. Every pixel is calibrated at the factory to Delta-E less than 1 — professional color accuracy out of the box.

Ecosystem

OmniOS Continuity

Use your OmniPhone as a webcam, answer calls from your wrist, or pick up where you left off on any screen. The entire OmniTech ecosystem stays in sync through OmniCloud.

Security

Touch ID + Secure Enclave

Touch ID in the keyboard unlocks your Mac and authorizes purchases in milliseconds. All biometric data is stored exclusively in the OmniChip Secure Enclave — never leaves the device.
