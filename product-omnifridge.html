Key Features

Your Kitchen, Managed.

Vision AI

4K Internal Inventory Camera

See inside your fridge from anywhere in the world via OmniPhone. OmniAI identifies every item, tracks quantities, and alerts you when something is running low or approaching expiry.

CoolSync

5-Zone Temperature Control

Five independently controlled zones maintain the optimal temperature for every food category — meats at 34°F, produce at 38°F, wine at 55°F, all simultaneously and precisely.

OmniHome

Energy Automation

OmniHome integrates the fridge into energy routines — pre-cooling before the door is opened, shifting defrost cycles to off-peak electricity hours, and coordinating with OmniTherm for whole-home energy optimization.

Ice

Dual Ice — Cubes + Nugget

Two independent ice makers produce standard cubes for drinks and OmniCrush nugget ice for cocktails and smoothies. Both types are always available simultaneously.

Matter

Universal Smart Home Compatibility

Matter 1.2 and Thread ensure OmniFridge works with any smart home platform — not just OmniHome. Apple Home, Google Home, and Amazon Alexa all supported natively.

Efficiency

ENERGY STAR Most Efficient

Advanced compressor and insulation technology uses ~480 kWh annually — less than the average laptop left on standby. OmniHome tracks energy usage in real time and suggests optimization routines.
