Key Features

The Spatial Computer.

Display

4K Per-Eye Micro-OLED

33 megapixels total — more pixels than a 4K television, rendered simultaneously for both eyes at 120 Hz. Individual pixels are invisible at normal VR distances, eliminating the "screen door effect" completely.

OmniOS Spatial

Unified OS — Not a Walled Garden

OmniOS Spatial is the same OS kernel as OmniPhone and OmniBook — extended for 3D space. Your OmniCloud data, OmniAI context, and OmniDev apps run natively in VR without porting or re-purchasing.

Tracking

Eye-Tracked Foveated Rendering

OmniVR renders full 4K only where your eyes are actually focused and reduces detail in peripheral regions — cutting GPU load by 40% while the display looks sharper than full-resolution rendering without foveation.

Passthrough

Full-Color Mixed Reality

Four passthrough cameras deliver sub-12ms full-color video of the real world — fast enough to walk, pour a drink, or read a physical document without removing the headset.

Hand Tracking

Markerless Finger Precision

OmniChip's spatial NPU tracks all 27 joints in each hand at 60 Hz — no gloves, no controllers required. Type on a virtual keyboard, pinch to select, or gesture to navigate naturally.

Ecosystem

OmniBook as Spatial Compute

Connect OmniVR to OmniBook via Wi-Fi 6E or USB 4.0 to offload rendering to M2 Max — unlocking GPU performance that delivers professional-grade 3D visualization and spatial design applications.
