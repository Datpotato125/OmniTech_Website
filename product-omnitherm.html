Key Features

Climate That Learns You.

OmniAI

7-Day Schedule Learning

Within a week of installation, OmniAI builds a complete heating and cooling schedule from your behavior — no manual programming required. It knows when you wake up, leave, return, and sleep.

Occupancy

mmWave Presence Detection

Unlike PIR sensors that only detect motion, OmniTherm's mmWave radar detects the presence of a stationary person — so a room with someone sitting still and reading is never incorrectly classified as empty.

Energy

Utility Rate Optimization

OmniTherm reads your local utility's time-of-use rate schedule and shifts heavy HVAC loads to off-peak hours automatically — reducing electricity bills by an average of 23% in the first year.

Air Quality

VOC + CO2 + PM2.5 Monitoring

Three air quality sensors track volatile organic compounds, CO2 concentration, and fine particulate matter in real time — alerting you when ventilation or filtration is needed for health.

Geofencing

Arrive-Home Pre-Conditioning

OmniTherm tracks your OmniPhone location and begins heating or cooling 20 minutes before your estimated arrival — so the house is at the perfect temperature the moment you walk in.

Resilience

Full Offline Operation

Unlike competitors that require cloud connectivity, OmniTherm continues its learned schedule indefinitely with no internet — powered by the 72-hour backup battery if the power goes out.
