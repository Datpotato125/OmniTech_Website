Key Features

Precision Cleaning.

SpraySync

Zone-Targeted Water Routing

SpraySync maps the interior and directs water pressure to where dishes are actually loaded — not a uniform spray pattern. Burnt-on casserole dish at the back? The system knows and focuses pressure there.

OmniAI

Soil-Adaptive Cycles

An optical turbidity sensor reads actual soil concentration in the wash water and adjusts cycle intensity, temperature, and duration in real time — using only the resources each specific load actually needs.

Drying

CrystalDry Zeolite Technology

Natural zeolite minerals absorb moisture and generate heat from a chemical reaction — drying plastics and bowls completely without a heating element, using zero additional electricity.

Noise

38 dBA — Barely Audible

At 38 dBA, OmniDish is quieter than a library. Run it overnight, during dinner conversation, or while a baby sleeps in the next room — you won't hear it.

Sanitize

NSF/ANSI 184 Certified

Steam Sanitize program reaches 167°F and eliminates 99.999% of bacteria — certified for use in households where immunocompromised individuals require clinical-level sanitization.

OmniHome

Kitchen Automation

OmniHome coordinates OmniDish with OmniFridge meal planning — when a recipe is completed, OmniDish pre-stages the appropriate program and alerts you to load dishes immediately.
