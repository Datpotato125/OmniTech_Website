Key Features

Health. Fitness. Independence.

Health

ECG + SpO2 + Temperature

FDA-cleared ECG detects atrial fibrillation. SpO2 monitoring tracks blood oxygen through the night. Wrist temperature sensor reveals trends in recovery and menstrual cycle prediction.

Safety

Crash & Fall Detection

A high-g accelerometer detects vehicle collisions. The gyroscope detects hard falls. In both cases, OmniWatch prompts you — and if unresponsive, automatically calls emergency services and shares your GPS location.

Navigation

Dual-Frequency GPS

L1 + L5 dual-frequency GPS is the most accurate positioning system available in a consumer watch. Routes are accurate to within 1 meter — critical for trail running, cycling, and urban navigation.

Independence

LTE Cellular

The cellular OmniWatch model operates completely independently of your OmniPhone — stream music, take calls, get directions, and receive notifications with your phone left at home.

OmniAI

Wrist-Based AI Assistant

Raise your wrist and speak naturally. OmniAI on OmniWatch handles reminders, messages, smart home controls, and context-aware suggestions without needing your phone nearby.

Payments

OmniPay NFC

Pay at any NFC terminal without your phone or wallet. OmniPay is secured by the OmniChip Secure Enclave — your card data is never stored on the device or transmitted to merchants.
