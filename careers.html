Why OmniTech

Work on Problems That Have Never Been Solved.

OmniTech is the only company attempting to unify personal devices, home appliances, immersive computing, and a common AI platform under one OS. The engineering and design challenges here do not exist anywhere else.

01

Genuinely Novel Work

Designing an OS kernel that runs identically on a smartwatch, a dishwasher, and a VR headset. Building AI inference pipelines that work entirely on-device without a cloud connection. These problems have no off-the-shelf answers.

02

Direct Product Impact

We are a 120-person company. Every engineer, designer, and researcher works directly on products that will ship to consumers. There is no bureaucratic distance between your work and what people hold in their hands.

03

Engineering-First Culture

Six-week development cycles. Rigorous QA. Quarterly roadmap reviews that engineers participate in directly. We build things that last and we take craft seriously at every level of the stack.

04

Ownership & Equity

Early-stage equity with significant upside. Competitive base compensation benchmarked against top-tier technology companies. We share the outcome with the people who build it.

05

Sustainability by Design

Modular, repair-friendly hardware is a core engineering constraint — not a marketing afterthought. We are targeting carbon-neutral operations and building right-to-repair into every product line.

06

Transparent Leadership

All-hands every two weeks. Published internal OKRs. Founders who are engineers first. We operate on the OMNI values — Openness, Mastery, Nearness, Innovation — at every level of the organization.

Divisions

Where You Will Work.

OmniTech operates across eight vertical engineering divisions and a set of horizontal platform programs that span every product category.

DIVISION 01

Hardware Engineering

Silicon design, thermal engineering, mechanical systems, repairability architecture, battery optimization.

DIVISION 02

Software & OS

OmniOS kernel, continuity engine, device bridge, UI framework across all form factors.

DIVISION 03

AI Research & Engineering

On-device inference, federated learning, NLU, contextual intelligence, OmniAI platform.

DIVISION 04

Cloud & Infrastructure

OmniCloud platform, real-time sync, global infrastructure, zero-trust security architecture.

DIVISION 05

Smart Home & Appliances

OmniHome platform, appliance firmware, automation engine, energy optimization systems.

DIVISION 06

Immersive Computing

OmniVR / OmniGlass hardware, spatial computing SDK, display systems, tracking algorithms.

DIVISION 07

Design

Industrial design, UX/UI across all platforms, design systems, accessibility, brand identity.

DIVISION 08

Operations, Marketing & Enterprise

Supply chain, manufacturing, go-to-market, enterprise sales, developer relations, finance.
