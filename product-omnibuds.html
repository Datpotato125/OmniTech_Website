Key Features

Hear Everything. Block Everything.

ANC

Adaptive -40 dB Cancellation

OmniChip H1 samples ambient noise 48,000 times per second and generates an equal and opposite anti-noise signal. Hybrid feedforward and feedback microphones tackle noise from all directions simultaneously.

Spatial Audio

Head-Tracked Dolby Atmos

A 6-axis IMU in each bud tracks head position in real time, anchoring audio to a fixed point in space. OmniAI builds a personalized HRTF profile from an ear scan for the most accurate spatial rendering available.

OmniAI

On-Device Voice Assistant

Say "Hey Omni" to access your full OmniAI assistant — set reminders, control smart home devices, get directions, and dictate messages, all processed locally on OmniChip H1.

Battery

36-Hour Total Battery

6 hours in bud and 30 more in the case gives you a full work week of commutes without recharging the case. 5 minutes of case charge delivers a full hour of playback when you're in a rush.

Audio

OmniLossless 96 kHz Codec

When paired with an OmniPhone or OmniBook, OmniBuds streams lossless audio at up to 96 kHz / 24-bit over Bluetooth — resolution previously reserved for wired headphones.

Ecosystem

Instant Device Switching

OmniBuds switch automatically between your OmniPhone, OmniBook, and iPad the moment you start audio on a new device. Multipoint lets two devices connect simultaneously — work laptop and phone, simultaneously.
