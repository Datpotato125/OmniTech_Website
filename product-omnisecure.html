Key Features

Secure. Private. Yours.

Privacy

Zero-Knowledge Local Storage

All footage is encrypted with your OmniID key before it touches the OmniSecure Hub's SSD. OmniTech has no ability to access your recordings — not even for support or legal requests without your key.

OmniAI

On-Device Facial Recognition

Face recognition runs entirely on OmniChip inside the hub — no facial data is sent to the cloud. Register trusted faces in the OmniHome app and receive alerts only for unrecognized individuals.

No Fees

$0/Month, Always

Every feature — 4K recording, AI detection, facial recognition, 60-day retention, two-way audio — works with local storage at no subscription cost. Cloud backup is optional, not required.

Video

4K HDR Color Night Vision

Full-color night vision at 40 feet uses low-light sensors and OmniAI enhancement — not just infrared black-and-white. Read license plates and identify clothing colors in complete darkness.

Automation

Detection-Triggered Scenes

When OmniSecure detects a person at the front door, OmniHome can automatically turn on porch lights, unlock the door for recognized faces, and send a push notification — all in under 300ms.

Scale

Up to 16 Cameras Per Hub

Start with 2 cameras and expand to 16 as your needs grow. The OmniSecure Hub handles all AI processing locally — performance doesn't degrade as more cameras are added.
